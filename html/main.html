About Us

It all started back in 2003 when Magnus was in Sri Lanka for studies, and he first discovered the magic of custom-made dress shirts. An idea was born; why not offer this concept to the large masses? Custom-made clothing was nothing new back then, but nobody had revolutionized the industry in ages. His friend Nalin set up their first production and still holds the title of Managing Director to this day. Back home in Sweden, he and Mats-Ola put the idea to the test. You guessed right; it was a good one.Our head office is located in Helsingborg in the south of Sweden with currently more than 20 employees living and breathing custom clothing and innovation. Our company in Sri Lanka, owned by our Swedish corporation, now has over 500 employees in our two facilities located in Alawwa and Maho
